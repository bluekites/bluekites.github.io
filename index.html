FAQs

Who are you?

Full-time web developer during the day, freelancer by night, and gym enthusiast in my off hours. Living day by day with one creative project at a time.

Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I have always been interested in the tech field. I have experience in many different programming languages and my most recent venture is the Ruby programming language created by Yukihiro Matsumoto. I fell in love with the simplicity and elegance of the language on first use and have never looked back. The transition into web development seems natural seeing as how Ruby on Rails is still an extremely relevant framework in the field of web development. Another important reason is that I feel like every piece of my work in the industry is akin to putting my mark in modern history, which I find to be fascinating as well as motivating.

What are your credentials?

Starting from my roots in biological sciences moving onto organic chemistry and financial services, I have always been a very motivated individual and achieved success. My venture into the web development field involves mostly studies through intensive bootcamps such as Thinkful along with a multitude of curated materials from industry professionals. I believe the best way to showcase my potential is through my past work which is all located right above in my portfolio!

Can you do some graphical designs for me?

No! Unfortunately I am not very artistically inclined. The design work that I am more familiar with is on the user experience side. This is a type of design that is about more than just making something pretty. It is about creating elegant products that users will not only want, but need. It is a design philosophy that is rooted in truly understanding the users and utilizing that to maximize the usability and efficacy of your products. That is the type of design that I do.

Why should I trust you with a project?

Because after seeing the results, you will be glad that you did.
